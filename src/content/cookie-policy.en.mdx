# Cookie Policy

**Version:** 1.0.0 &nbsp;·&nbsp; **Effective date:** 22 April 2026

This page complements the [Privacy Policy](/en/privacy). It lists every cookie and cookie-like storage mechanism used by **lolsensei.com**, the legal basis under which we set it, and how long it lasts.

You can accept, reject, or customise non-essential cookies at any time from the footer link **Cookie preferences**.

## 1. How consent works

- We never set analytics or marketing cookies before you give consent.
- Strictly-necessary cookies are always active; they do not require consent (Directive 2002/58/EC Art. 5(3)).
- Scrolling is not consent. Closing the banner is equivalent to rejecting non-essential cookies.
- A MAJOR version bump of this policy triggers a re-consent prompt.

### 2.1 Strictly necessary

| Name | Purpose | Duration | Party |
|---|---|---|---|
| `lolsensei_consent` | Stores your cookie preferences (JSON: `technical`, `analytics`, `marketing`) | 6 months | First-party |

| `jwt` (localStorage) | Session token after sign-in | Session / explicit sign-out | First-party |
| `csrf` (session) | Cross-site request forgery protection | Session | First-party |

### 2.2 Analytics (opt-in)

| Name | Purpose | Duration | Party |
|---|---|---|---|
| Cloudflare Web Analytics | Aggregate traffic metrics (page views, country, device class) | **Cookieless** — no browser cookie | First-party via `static.cloudflareinsights.com` |

Cloudflare Web Analytics is configured to run **without cookies**. It loads only if you grant analytics consent.

### 2.3 Marketing

We do not currently run marketing cookies. If this changes, the cookie banner will re-prompt.

## 3. Third-party services (no cookies on lolsensei.com)

The following processors are used on different domains (checkout, billing, AI inference). They may set their own cookies when you interact with their flows:

- **Stripe Payments Europe Ltd.** — billing; cookies set on `checkout.stripe.com` only.
- **Google Inc.** — OAuth sign-in; cookies set on `accounts.google.com` only.
- **Anthropic PBC** — AI coaching inference; no cookies, API-only.

## 4. How to manage cookies

- **In-product**: use the **Cookie preferences** button in the footer at any time.
- **Browser settings**: Chrome, Firefox, Safari and Edge all let you block or delete cookies. This may degrade technical functionality.
- **Do Not Track**: we honour the `GPC` (Global Privacy Control) signal by defaulting analytics and marketing to off.

## 5. Questions

Email **privacy@lolsensei.com** — we respond within 30 days (GDPR Art. 12(3)).
